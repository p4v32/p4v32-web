the idea

the idea is mixing characters from text
with decoyls and duplicit decoyls.
the duplicit docoyls amount is
lower than decoyls

with out duplicit decoyls short texts can
be find because some of decoyls are
same as charactrers from original text

how it works

simplefied "map" of password encrypt

how seed is generated

the pasword gets splited to invividual characters.
characters are compared with list of posible characters.
every posible character has a position in the list (list[5])
when character is = to character in posible characters the
position in posible charctes is used as number and its appended
to num_output list. seed = num_output + gen seed. gen seed is pre
defined value. if you change gen seed its can be decrypte only
with same gen seed modification

how key is generated

1. for characters is generated set of tkeys (tkey)
2. fkeys (fkey) are generated
